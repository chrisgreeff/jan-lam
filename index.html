About Me

A successful, results oriented, self-motivated, well qualified and experienced chartered accountant with over 13 years’ experience including business analysis, project management, financial re-engineering, business performance management, financial control and financial planning and analysis.

Has over 10 years’ experience gained in the financial services sector, including 6.5 years obtained in London. Has significant experience in financial planning, analysis, controlling, decision support systems and business process re-engineering, coupled with very good full life-cycle implementation experience in financial planning systems, including Hyperion Planning and Oracle® HFM/FDM as well as finance process outsourcing and regulatory reporting projects.

Experiences have been gained in a number of large banking and financial services enterprises both in New Zealand and London.
